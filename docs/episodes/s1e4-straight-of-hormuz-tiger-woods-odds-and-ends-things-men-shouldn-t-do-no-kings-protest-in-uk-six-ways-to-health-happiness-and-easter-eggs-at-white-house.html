S1E4: Straight of Hormuz; Tiger Woods; Odds and Ends (Things Men Shouldn’t Do, No Kings Protest In UK, Six Ways To Health/Happiness and Easter Eggs at White House)

Where headlines breathe and the conversation is always on tap.

Description

In this episode, we cover a wide range of topics that hit everything from global tension to everyday common sense. We break down the strategic importance of the **Strait of Hormuz**—why it matters, who controls it, and what happens if things go sideways. Then we shift gears to **Tiger Woods**, looking at his latest developments and what his legacy means at this stage of his career. From there, it’s a rapid-fire round of “odds and ends.” We call out **things men should absolutely avoid doing** (some obvious, some not), take a look at the **“No Kings” protest in the UK** and what’s driving it, and run through **six practical ways to improve your health and overall happiness** without overcomplicating it. We wrap things up on a lighter note with a look at the tradition of **Easter eggs at the White House**—a mix of history, culture, and a bit of fun. A little serious, a little opinionated, and a little all over the place—just how we like it.
